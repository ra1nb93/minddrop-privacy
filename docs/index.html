Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

MindDrop is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

MindDrop collects anonymous usage data through Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app. This includes events such as sessions started, sessions completed, and drops saved. No personally identifiable information is collected.

All personal data — including your breathing sessions and drop journal entries — is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

Anonymous analytics data is used solely to understand how users interact with the app and to improve the user experience. We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Choices

You can disable analytics collection at any time from the app Settings. When disabled, no usage data will be collected or sent to Firebase.

Third-Party Services

MindDrop uses Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) for anonymous usage tracking. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Data Retention

Your journal entries and session history are stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. Anonymous analytics data is retained by Firebase for up to 14 months.

Medical Disclaimer

MindDrop is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical condition. Consult your doctor before starting any breathing practice if you have a medical condition.

Children's Privacy

MindDrop is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
